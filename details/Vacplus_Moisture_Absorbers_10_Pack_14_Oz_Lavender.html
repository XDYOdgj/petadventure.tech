Vacplus Moisture Absorbers

Keep your belongings safe and fresh with Vacplus Moisture Absorbers. Designed to combat excess humidity in your home, these 14 oz dehumidifier bags use advanced spherical calcium chloride to swiftly remove moisture and protect your space from dampness.

Key Features

Spherical Calcium Chloride: Our dehumidifier bags are loaded with high-quality spherical calcium chloride to ensure rapid and efficient moisture absorption, keeping your space drier for longer.

One-Way Moisture Permeable Paper: The specially designed paper allows moisture to be drawn in while keeping your items safe from any potential contamination or backflow.

500 ml Capacity: Each pack contains a large 500 ml moisture-absorbing capacity, offering long-term protection without the need for frequent replacements.

Lavender Fragrance: Infused with a subtle lavender scent, these packets not only control humidity but also freshen your air, leaving your home with a pleasant and natural aroma.

Compact & Reusable Design: The compact size makes it easy to hang in closets, bathrooms, or any small space. Plus, they are reusable and safe, making them an eco-friendly and sustainable choice.

Multi-Use Solution: Whether you're preserving clothes, documents, or electronics, Vacplus dehumidifier bags are a reliable, low-maintenance option. They are perfect for use in closets, basements, RVs, and even refrigerators.

Why Choose Vacplus?

Vacplus Moisture Absorbers offer a simple and effective solution to humidity control. Their convenient design and safe, non-toxic ingredients make them ideal for all family members and pets. With an elegant lavender scent and no messy water, they bring a fresh and clean feel to your home without the hassle of traditional dehumidifiers.
